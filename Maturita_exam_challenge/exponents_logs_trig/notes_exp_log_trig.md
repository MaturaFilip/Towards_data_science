#### Type 1

- same base equation with multiples and division

- for instance all this is base of 2

$$
2^{5x+2} \cdot \frac{1}{4} = 32^{x+2} \cdot (\frac{1}{4})^{2x+2}
$$

#### Type 2

- sabe base addition and subtraction

- put same base to left and other to right, or vice verse

- then we factor it

$$
3^{x+2} - 3^{3+1} = 189 - 3^x
$$

#### Type 3

- two different bases

$$
5 \cdot 3^{x-2} - 3 \cdot 5^{x+1} = 4 \cdot 5^{x+2} - 6 \cdot 3^x + 10 \cdot 5 ^x\\[2ex]

(\frac{5}{3})^{-3} = (\frac{5}{3})^{x}
$$

- one side one base, other side other base

- factor like type 2

#### Type 4

- substitution (when you have for instance 3^2x, 3^x)

$$
3^x \cdot (3^x - 3) + 2 = 2 \cdot (3^x - 2)
$$

- for instance

$$
(\frac{3}{2})^3 = a
$$

#### Type 5

- with log

$$
5^x = 7\\[2ex]

log_55^x = log_57\\[2ex]

x = log_57\\[2ex]

$$

# Log

- Logaritmus (y) je číslo, na které musíme umocnit základ (a), abychom dostali číslo logaritmované (x)

$$
y = log_ax\\[2ex]

a^y = x
$$

Na jaké číslo musím umocnit 2 abych dostal 4?

- 2 protože $2^2 = 4$

$$
log_2 4 =2
$$

Rules

$$
log_aA + log_aB = log_a(A \cdot B)\\[2ex]


a^A \cdot a^B = a^{A+B}\\[2ex]

log_aA - log_aB = log_a(\frac{A}{B})\\[2ex]


log_aX^K = K \cdot log_aX\\[2ex]

log_aX = \frac{log_bX}{log_ba}\\[2ex]

log_35 = \frac{log 5}{log 3}
$$

#### Inequations

- if base is les than 1 (for instance $log_\frac{1}{2}$), we have to switch < or >
